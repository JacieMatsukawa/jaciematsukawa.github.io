About

Jacie Matsukawa is a Certified Meeting Professional based in Honolulu.

By day, Jacie works as a meetings and events manager for the Pacific Telecommunications Council (PTC). In her role, she coordinates logistics for in-person, virtual, and hybrid conferences and events as well as oversees the organization’s annual conference program and stage management. In addition, Jacie administers and implements all aspects of the accreditation process for the PTC Academy online training courses.

Outside of work, Jacie enjoys staying active from running to hiking to yoga to paddleboarding to long walks in the park; escaping to nature; spending time with family, friends, and pets; traveling to new places and creating new experiences; growing her plant collection; experimenting in the kitchen and sharing a meal; service to others; and finding different ways to keep her mind happy and focused in the present.
